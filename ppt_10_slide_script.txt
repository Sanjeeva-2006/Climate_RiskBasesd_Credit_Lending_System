Slide 2 - Problem Statement (Speaker: Sanjeeva, 55 sec)
On slide:
- Traditional lending checks credit and income
- Climate risk is often ignored
- Long-term property risk affects repayment and asset value

Speak exactly:
"Current loan underwriting mainly focuses on applicant financial history.
But property-level climate risk is often not modeled clearly.
That means lenders may approve loans in high flood, cyclone, heat, or coastal-risk areas without long-term risk visibility.
Our project addresses this gap with explainable climate-aware lending support."

Speak exactly:
"For this presentation, this is our official project input and output definition.

Output has four parts.
First, property-level Climate Credit Score.
Second, Loan Pricing Adjustment.
Third, Portfolio Risk Alert.
Fourth, Explainability Log that clearly explains why the score was generated."

Slide 6 - Core Logic Behind the 4 Outputs (Speaker: Sanjeeva, 75 sec)
On slide:
- Climate feature extraction per property location
- Long-term risk projection
- Scoring and policy mapping
- Explainability generation

Speak exactly:
"Our backend converts geolocation plus climate projections into four outputs.
First, it extracts climate risk features for each property location.
Then it computes long-term risk trends.
Next, it maps risk to Climate Credit Score and Loan Pricing Adjustment rules.
At portfolio level, it generates Portfolio Risk Alert summaries.
Finally, it produces an Explainability Log that states the major climate factors behind the result."
